The Café Right Off the Ferry

If you're a Singaporean planning a quick getaway to Batam, chances are you're taking the ferry into Harbour Bay. And the moment you step off that ferry, you're already two minutes from one of Batam's most talked-about cafés.

Melt Me Dessert & Coffee Space is halal-certified, specialty-coffee-forward, and home to the OG Cheezmelt — Batam's most photographed Basque burnt cheesecake.

Practical Tips for Singapore Visitors

⛴Ferry route: Tanah Merah → Harbour Bay (approx. 50 min) or Harbourfront → Harbour Bay
💴Currency: Bring SGD cash to exchange at Harbour Bay money changers — better rates than airport
🕐Best time: Weekday mornings are quieter. Weekends lively — especially after 2 PM
☕First order: OG Cheezmelt + any espresso drink. Start there.
