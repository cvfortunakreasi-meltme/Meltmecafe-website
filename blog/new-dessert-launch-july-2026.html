Something New at Melt Me

This July, Melt Me is expanding beyond the OG Cheezmelt lineup with seven new creations — spanning fudgy brownies, a Dubai-inspired chocolate crush, and four new takes on ube. Each one is made fresh in-house, following the same halal-certified process as the rest of the menu.

If you've visited before, this is the moment to come back and try something outside the original six. If it's your first time, these new items make just as strong a starting point as the classics.

The Full Lineup

01
Brownie Madness A dense, fudgy brownie loaded with toppings — rich and deliberately indulgent.
02
Dubai Berry Crush Our take on the viral Dubai chocolate style, reworked with a berry profile instead of pistachio-chocolate.
03
Ube Cheezmelt The signature soft-baked Basque burnt cheesecake, reimagined in purple yam (ube).
04
Ube Cheesecup A crunchy tart shell filled with an ultra-melty ube cheese centre — the cup version of our Cheesecup line.
05
Pistachio Cheesecup Same crunchy shell and melty centre, built around pistachio instead of ube.
06
Matcha Ube An unlikely pairing — ceremonial-grade matcha layered against ube — that turns out to work.
07
Ube Chewy Cheezmelt A chewier, denser take on the Cheezmelt format, finished with an ube centre.

Where to Start

With seven new items, deciding what to order first can be its own challenge. A few starting points depending on what you're in the mood for:

🍫Chocolate lovers: start with Brownie Madness or Dubai Berry Crush
💜Ube first-timers: Ube Cheezmelt is the closest to our original OG Cheezmelt in texture
🍵Something unexpected: Matcha Ube is the one guests ask about most
🧁Smaller portion: Ube Cheesecup or Pistachio Cheesecup, both single-serve

"Made fresh in-house, following the same process as everything else on the menu — no shortcuts on the new items either."

Ada yang Baru di Melt Me

Bulan Juli ini, Melt Me memperluas menu di luar deretan OG Cheezmelt dengan tujuh kreasi baru — mulai dari brownie fudgy, chocolate crush ala Dubai, hingga empat variasi baru berbahan ube. Semuanya dibuat fresh di dapur kami sendiri, mengikuti proses halal-certified yang sama seperti menu lainnya.

Kalau kamu sudah pernah datang, ini saatnya balik lagi dan coba sesuatu di luar enam rasa original. Kalau ini kunjungan pertamamu, menu baru ini juga jadi titik awal yang sama kuatnya dengan menu klasik.
