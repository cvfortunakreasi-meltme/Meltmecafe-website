Where to Find the Best Dessert in Batam

If there's one dessert in Batam that keeps getting mentioned — by locals, by Singapore visitors, by food bloggers — it's the OG Cheezmelt at Melt Me Dessert & Coffee Space.

It's a Basque burnt cheesecake: soft-baked, caramelized on top, and melt-in-the-mouth in the centre. Simple in concept, but the execution is what's made it one of the most photographed desserts on the island.

The OG Cheezmelt

Unlike a traditional cheesecake, the Basque burnt style skips the crust entirely. The result is a deeply caramelized exterior with a centre that stays soft, almost custard-like. Made fresh in-house daily, available in six flavours.
