The Cheezmelt芝士蛋糕Cheezmelt

Not your ordinary cheesecake. 不是你普通的芝士蛋糕。 Bukan cheesecake biasa.

Melt Me's signature is a soft-baked basque cheesecake square — caramelized on top, impossibly silky inside. Available in six flavours, made fresh in-house daily.
